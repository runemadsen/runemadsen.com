Rune Madsen

I am a designer, artist, and educator who use programming languages to create things with the computer. As a co-founder of Design Systems International, I specialise in code-based design projects including non-trivial interfaces, custom design tools, design systems, and dynamic branding projects. I am also the author of Programming Design Systems and Rune.js. I believe in simplicity, organized complexity, and that the pragmatic and poetic is inseparable.
